Top 30 hobbyist parts (non-sensor) for ESP32/Arduino:

3. WS2812B / NeoPixel addressable LED strip
4. 7-segment display module (TM1637)
5. 8x8 LED matrix (MAX7219)
6. Standard LEDs assortment + resistors

**Motors & Movement**
7. SG90 / MG90S micro servo
8. 28BYJ-48 stepper motor + ULN2003 driver
9. DC gear motors (TT motors)
10. L298N or DRV8833 motor driver
11. A4988 / TMC2208 stepper driver
12. Vibration motor module

**Power**
13. Breadboard power supply module (MB102)
14. 18650 battery holder + cells
15. TP4056 lithium charging module
16. Buck converter (LM2596)
17. Boost converter (MT3608)
18. AMS1117 3.3V regulator module

**Input & Control**
19. Tactile push buttons
20. Rotary encoder module (KY-040)
21. Potentiometers (10k)
22. 4x4 membrane keypad
23. Joystick module
24. IR remote + receiver kit

**Switching & Output**
25. Relay module (1/2/4 channel)
26. MOSFET module (IRF520)
27. Passive/active buzzer
28. Solid state relay (SSR)

**Connectivity & Storage**
29. MicroSD card reader module
30. Logic level shifter (3.3V ↔ 5V)

**Bonus essentials**
